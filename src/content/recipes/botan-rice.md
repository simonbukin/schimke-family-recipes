Ingredients:

- Rice, 1.5 cups 
- Water, 2 cups 

Cookware:

- Small pot, 1
- Rice fluffer, 1

Steps:

- Put dry rice in pot.
- Fill with cold water until just above the rice.
- Stir with hand. 
- Drain water.
- Repeat one more time.
- Add two cups of water to the washed rice.
- Bring rice to boil, uncovered.
- Once boiling, place lid on pot and turn to low for 20 minutes.
- Fluff.
